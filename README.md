## New York University

[New York University](https://www.nyu.edu/) is a private institution that was founded in 1831. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 26,733, and the setting is Urban.

#### Admission Requirements

* High School GPA. Required.
* High School Rank. Recommended.
* High School Transcript. Required.
